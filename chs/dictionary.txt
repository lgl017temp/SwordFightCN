The amount of damage a Fighter can take before they lose the duel.  Total HP is Base HP * Multipliers: Rank, HP Segments and Armor being the primary multipliers (Partial HP Segments are counted: The Disciple gets +0.5 HP Segments per Rank, and 1/200th per level)=斗士在决斗失败之前可以承受的伤害量.  总生命值为基础生命值 * 乘数: 段位, 生命值段数和护甲是主要乘数 (部分生命值段数被计算在内: 弟子每段位获得 +0.5 生命值段数, 并且每级 +1/200)
Goes down with each action and when a Fighter is Damaged.  When Stamina reaches the Yellow segment Hit% and Def% begin to drop. In the Orange segment Special Attack % begins to fall and Attack Failure may occur (Chance of failing is Remaining Stamina/Max Stamina.  Fighters will rest to restore Stamina if both suffer from Attack Failure.)  In the Red segment Hit% and Def% drop after all other calculations=随着每个动作以及斗士受到伤害而下降.  当体力到达条的黄色部分时命中率和防御率开始下降. 在橙色区域特殊攻击率开始下降并且可能发生攻击失败 (失败的机率是剩余体/最大体力.  如果双方都发生攻击失败, 斗士将休息以恢复体力.)  在红色区域命中率和防御率在所有其他计算之后下降
The amount of normal damage that will be dealt when you hit the opponent.  Does not include any damage reduction effects the opponent may have.  Dual Swords damage depends on which weapon is used or if both are used in the attack=当你击中对手时将造成的正常伤害量.  不包括对手可能具有的任何减伤效果.  双剑伤害取决于使用哪种武器或是否在攻击中同时使用两种武器
The chance that a Fighter attacks.  Failing this roll means the fighter defends and the opponent attacks=斗士攻击的几率.  此掷骰失败意味着斗士进行防御, 而对手则进行攻击
The chance that an attack will deal damage=一次攻击造成伤害的几率
The chance to Block or Dodge an attack.  The final Def % is always the opposite of the Opponent's Hit chance=格挡或躲避攻击的机会.  最终的防御率总是与对手的命中率相反
The attacker's attack speed determines the speed of the animations.  Faster speeds means more Exp and Gold over time and better Top 8 Duel scores.=攻击者的攻击速度决定了动画的速度.  更快的速度意味着随着时间的推移会有更多的经验值和金币, 以及更好的前 8 决斗分数.
An attack that glances off armor or is partially defended: If the Attacker's Hit attempt is between Def% and Def% + Deflect% then the attack is Deflected and All Damage is reduced by 50%=掠过盔甲或部分防御的攻击: 如果攻击者的命中介于防御率和防御率 + 偏斜率之间, 则攻击被偏斜并且所有伤害降低 50%
Chance that an attack will deal additional damage equal to 1 of the opponent's Hp Segments=一次攻击有几率造成等同于对手 1 个生命值段的额外伤害
The chance a successfully defended attack is either Dodged, Blocked by a Sword or Blocked by a Shield (Dodge% is calculated first. Sword and Shield Block split the remainder then bonuses to Shield Block% are applied reducing/increasing Sword Block% as needed)=一次成功防御攻击的几率(被躲避, 被剑格挡或被盾牌格挡) (首先计算闪避率. 剑和盾牌格挡平分剩余部分, 然后应用盾牌格挡率的奖励, 根据需要减少/增加剑格挡率)
Represents the force behind an attack or block, affecting Damage Dealt (Attack Impact - Impact Resistance, Minimum 10%) and giving a chance to break-through Blocks, causing a Deflect (Attacker's Impact - Defender's Impact)=代表攻击或格挡背后的力量, 影响造成的伤害 (攻击冲击 - 冲击抗性, 最低 10%) 并有机会突破格挡, 造成偏斜 (攻击者的冲击 - 防御者的冲击)
